PURPOUSE OF XplorIndia
The XplorIndia Project aims to create a user-friendly digital platform. This guide provides detailed information about various aspects of the city, including: hotels,colleges,and tourist places

TECHNOLIGES USED
Frontend:->HTML,CSS,JAVASCRIPT
Backend:->JAVA ,SPRINGBOOT
Database:->MySQL
Build tool:-> MAVEN

OBJECTIVE
• Using this project, the details of city can be accessed from anywhere at any time.
• With the help of this website, students can look for the academic institutes located in the city.
• You can view the complete map of city using this project.
The XplorIndia Project aims to enhance the experience of exploring the city by providing a reliable, easy-to-use, and comprehensive resource. It stores details of a city and displays information about the city such as hotels, colleges, tourist places, and also some general information.This acts as a guide to the new visitors.
